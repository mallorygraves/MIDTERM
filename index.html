You dig your own Grave(s)

Solutions to get out of the hole that you dug for yourself

1. Don't give up

Everyone loses at least once in their life. Don't let that stop you from doing what you want to achieve in life.

2. Ask yourself how you ended up in this situation

Was this a situation that you brought yourself into, or were you pulled into the situation?

3.Is there any way you could have prevented this problem?

Could you have chosen a different path for the decisions you made?

ALWAYS REMEMBER TO TAKE FULL RESPONSIBILITY FOR YOUR ACTIONS.
